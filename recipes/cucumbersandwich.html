Cucumber Sandwich

Refreshing and minimalistic sandwich, perfect for summer time

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread
  • Cream cheese
  • Sliced cucumbers
  • Dill

Steps

  1. Toast the sour dough bread in your ninja oven
  2. Spread a light amount of cream cheese on both pieces of bread
  3. Place sliced cucumbers on sourdough
  4. sprinkle with dill
  5. optional:mix yourself a drink!

Enjoy!
