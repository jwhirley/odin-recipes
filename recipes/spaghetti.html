Spaghetti! Spaghetti!

The JD Staple: Spaghetti and Texas Toast

Ingredients

  • Sauce of choice
    • Kirland or Aldi Marinara Sauce
    • Homemade pesto sauce
    • Alfredo sauce on sale
  • Garlic Bread of choice
  • Whole Wheat Spaghetti Noodles
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Fresh grated Swiss cheese
  • Fresh graded parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until cooked
  2. Bake garlic bread in ninja oven
  3. Heat sauce in low pan
  4. Mix it all together, top with cheese and pepper

Enjoy!
